“Lucky Raiment” would like to introduce ourselves as a well established export oriented manufacturing industry and to emphasize our company's role in the supply of quality of Men’s t-shirt, ladies wear, children’s wear.We have been in the industry for the past 6 years which have taught us that business is not just about balance sheets. Our growth has been propelled by people – a principled Management as well as a passionate workforce. It is their dedication towards development that has taken our presence across the world.

We at Lucky Raiment , understand our client's need for quality and durability. With a strong team of dedicated professionals, we deliver products with a flawless finish. Our quality control team supervises each stage of the manufacturing process. Right from the procurement of raw materials, embellishments, designing and stitching to finishing, the products undergo stringent quality procedures.
